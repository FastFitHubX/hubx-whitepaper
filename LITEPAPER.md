# HubXCoin ($HUBX) Litepaper

## Introduction
HubXCoin ($HUBX) is the native utility coin of the FastFitHub ecosystem, a revolutionary Web3 platform designed to incentivize healthy lifestyles through a unique **Proof of Workout** mechanism. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies, $HUBX rewards physical activity, transforming effort into digital value.

## FastFitHub Ecosystem
FastFitHub integrates wearable technology, AI-powered coaching, and a decentralized marketplace to create a holistic fitness experience. Users engage in verified workouts, earn $HUBX, and utilize it for premium services, products, or governance within the ecosystem.

## Key Features
- **Proof of Workout**: Verifies physical activity for fair reward distribution.
- **Multi-Asset Rewards**: Earn $HUBX, with bonuses in Bitcoin ($BTC) and Dogecoin ($DOGE) for exceptional performance.
- **Decentralized Governance**: $HUBX holders participate in key decisions for ecosystem development.
- **Ecosystem Marketplace**: A platform for fitness-related goods and services.
